ABOUT US

Welcome to ASA Overseas! We are a dedicated education consultancy firm committed to helping students achieve their dreams of studying abroad. With offices located in Nagpur, Raipur, Bhopal, Indore, and Jabalpur, our team of expert consultants provides comprehensive guidance and support throughout your educational journey. From free counseling sessions to assist you in choosing the right university, to helping you craft a compelling Statement of Purpose and Letter of Recommendation, we are with you every step of the way. We specialize in facilitating admissions to top universities in the USA, UK, Australia, and Ireland, ensuring you receive the best opportunities for your future.
